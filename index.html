About Me


Dan Potts is new to the world of web development but brings a renewed vigor to the work. He has spent the previous 15 years working at the Service Employees International Union working as a labor organizer, representative, and negotiator. Dan will be using his newfound skills to engage union members on a digital platform and is spearheading the union's new initiative in signing up workers on union membership on electronic membership cards. He is also exploring the option of using his development skills in a freelance capacity, helping individuals and and organizations reach their goals.
